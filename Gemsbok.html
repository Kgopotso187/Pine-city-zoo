GEMSBOK

The gemsbok or gemsbuck (Oryx gazella) is a large antelope native to Southern Africa, particularly the Kalahari Desert.

It is featured on Namibia's coat of arms, with an estimated population of 373,000.

The name "gemsbok" is derived from Afrikaans and Dutch, referring to the male chamois.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the gemsboks just beyond panda canyon.
